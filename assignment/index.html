Few Facts About The World Which We Live

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, known as the "Blue Planet" because about 71% of its surface is covered in water. It is the only known planet to host life, supported by its atmosphere, liquid water,
and a balanced temperature. Earth's unique geology includes a "squishy" mantle that drives tectonic plate movement, causing earthquakes and volcanic activity.
